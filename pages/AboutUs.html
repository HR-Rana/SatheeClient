About us

Company History

Evidence based health span improving company of 21st century

Shathee is founded by a group of passionate healthcare professionals and tech enthusiasts who recognises the need for a transformative approach to healthcare. The two most common causes of death worldwide: heart disease and stroke which are entirely preventable with early intervention and proactive care. We identified that the traditional healthcare model, which focuses on treating illnesses rather than preventing them, is not adequately addressing these issues.


We established Shathee with the goal of creating a new model of healthcare centred on prevention. By leveraging cutting edge technology, expert medical knowledge, and evidence based strategies, we aim to provide accessible and high-quality preventive care to everyone on earth, starting with Bangladesh.

Mission

Our Mission

Our mission at Shathee is to build the most compelling evidence based health span improving company of the 21st century. We are dedicated to actively promoting positive behaviour change, preserving health, and preventing chronic diseases like diabetes and hypertension. We seek to empower individuals to take charge of their health and improve their quality of life through personalised support, accessible tele-health services, and regular monitoring of health markers.
Values

Our Values

Prioritising preventive measures to help patients avoid complications and enjoy healthier lives

Evidence Based Strategies

Grounded in scientific research and proven methodologies to ensure effectiveness

Ready Accessibility

Ensuring ready access to high quality preventive healthcare for everyone from everywhere.

Personalised Support

Tailoring care to address each patient's specific needs and goals

Continuous Improvement

Refining services and staying updated on the state of the art medical advancements
